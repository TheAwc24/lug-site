LUG Office Hours: Fall 2017

Our new office is Dana 216b. Our officers holds office hours every week, these office hours are for you to receive help with Linux outside of our tutoring sessions. If none of these office hours work for you, please contact us and we will try to work something out.

Our new office is behind not one but two card access doors. If you do not have card access, send us a message in the Slack #general channel. If someone is in the office, they can come out and let you in.
